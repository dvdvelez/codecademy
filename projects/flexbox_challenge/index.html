World's Softest Alpaca Yarn!

Alpaca yarn is a natural animal fiber that is soft, warm and non-abrasive. It is excellent for cold weather apparel and is hypoallergenic making it a good substitute for wool.

Our alpaca yarn is the world's finest and we pride ourselves in the authenticity and quality of our products. You will not feel anything softer!
